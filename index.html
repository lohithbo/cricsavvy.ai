Core Technology

Diode Laser Engraving System

Industrial-grade Diode laser with dynamic focus adjustment for curved surfaces. Achieves permanent marks on wood, leather, metal, and composite materials without compromising structural integrity.

  • Precision: 0.1mm accuracy
  • Depth Control: Variable 0.05-2mm
  • Speed: Up to 7000mm/s
  • Materials: 15+ surface types
Innovation

3D Surface Mapping

Advanced calibration system that maps non-linear surfaces in real-time, allowing perfect focal length maintenance across round handles, curved blades, and irregular shapes.

  • Mapping Speed: Real-time adjustment
  • Surface Types: Convex, concave, irregular
  • Accuracy: Sub-millimeter precision
  • Applications: Handles, stumps, gloves
